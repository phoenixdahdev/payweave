Stripe Surface A gains its billing-sync resource modules (PW-603): `payweave.stripe.customers` (create, retrieve, update, delete, list/iterate, search/iterateSearch), `payweave.stripe.products` (create, retrieve, update, delete, list/iterate, search/iterateSearch) and `payweave.stripe.prices` (create, retrieve, update, list/iterate, search/iterateSearch — prices have no delete endpoint; rotate by creating a new Price and archiving the old with `active: false`). Requests are validated with Zod before the form encoder runs and go to the wire as `application/x-www-form-urlencoded` bracket notation (array query filters like `ids`/`lookup_keys` use explicit indices); responses are loose bare resources (drift is logged, never thrown). All amounts (`unit_amount`, `balance`) are integer minor units. Lists paginate with `starting_after`/`has_more` cursor iterators; search endpoints use Stripe's token-based search pagination (`page`/`next_page`) with their own `iterateSearch` iterators. `create`/`update` accept an `idempotencyKey` option wired to Stripe's `Idempotency-Key` header (which also makes those POSTs retry-eligible). Delete/archive semantics are documented per PW-803's sync flows: customer deletion is permanent and cancels subscriptions; products with prices attached cannot be deleted (archive instead); price amount/currency/product/recurring are immutable after creation.
